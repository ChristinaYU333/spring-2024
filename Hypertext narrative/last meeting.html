The Last Meeting

The evening unfolded like the perfect date they'd always imagined. Max and Jo dined at a quaint, cozy restaurant, their laughter mingling with the soft clinking of glasses. The world outside seemed to fade away as they savored each moment, each bite, each shared glance.

After dinner, the night took them to a lively bar. The music pulsed, a vibrant backdrop to stories told and memories made. It was a night filled with joy, a temporary bubble of happiness that made everything else seem distant.

As the morning approached, the city bathed in a soft, early light, Max offered Jo a ride back to her hotel. Jo, wanting to cling to the independence that had always defined her, gently refused. It was then that Max, offered his jacket to Jo. "Keep this," he said, his voice carrying an unspoken weight.

In that moment, as Jo held the jacket close, a realization washed over her. Despite the perfection of their last date, this might truly be the end of their journey together. The jacket, warm and comforting, felt like a silent goodbye, a keepsake of what had been and what might never be again.
